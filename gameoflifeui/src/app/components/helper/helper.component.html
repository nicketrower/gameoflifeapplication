Tips and Tricks

When the game is stopped, you can draw figures on the playing field yourself. There are several options for this:

a) paint individual cells

Clicking on a cell changes its state. If it is a living cell (black) it becomes a dead cell (white) and vice versa.

b) paint rows

If you click on a cell while holding down alt, the entire row starting from that cell will become black (living cell).

If you click on a cell while holding down alt + shift, then the entire row from that cell onwards will become white (dead cells).

c) paint columns

If you click on a cell while holding down ctrl (Strg on German Keyboard), the entire column starting from that cell will become black (living cell).

If you click on a cell while holding down ctr + shift, then the entire row from that cell onwards will become white (dead cells).

d) add predefined object/patterns

Use context menu (right click) on the board an choose a pattern.
